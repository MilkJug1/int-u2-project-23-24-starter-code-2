Why Open Source Software Matters and Your Freedom is Important


Open Source Software, What it is and Why you should care.

Section 1

Open Source software is software where it's code is freely available and open to anyone contributing. Open source is very important to how certain apps, programs, and even games function. Unfortunately, people don't know about what open source software, and think it's bad to use since anyone can contribute.

Open source is really important, it's applications that power other applications, stuff that respects settings and privacy, and it is always something you can trust(Unless it's an open source app that's backed by a company). Most open source apps collect no data whatsoever, if any do, they will always ask instead of taking. And will respect your option and not collect any, or anonymize the data. Plus, they know what their users want, and listen to them, unlike other companies (Looking at you Adobe)

Now, if one wants to join and try out more open-source software, I recommend looking up alternatives of your favorite program. In fact, heres a github of alternatives to popular software, Click here for the List!
